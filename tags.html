Understanding Medicare

If you're approaching your Medicare transition or have experienced it from helping a parent, you know that Medicare is nothing short of CONFUSING! CMS gives you a fat booklet that's supposed to teach you everything you need to know, but frankly it's not an easy read. On top of that, you have every friend, neighbor, family member, and their dog giving you their opinion on what to do for your situation, AND you have no shortage of advertisements and mail just to throw fuel on the fire. Everyone's situation is unique, and we understand that it is extremely overwhelming to have all of this pressure thrown on you. That's where we come in!

Our Solution

The Medicare Masterclass was designed to have a no-pressure, non-intrusive approach to educate you about your Medicare and what you need to do for your situation. We've combined information from many verified, reputable sources and years of experience to break up what you need to know into manageable "Lessons" that you can watch on your own time. Each lesson takes about 9-12 minutes to complete, allowing you to learn at your own pace, when YOU want to!

Introduction

What You'll Learn:

  • What Original Medicare is and how it works.
  • The types of Supplemental Coverage available to you.
  • Specifics about Medicare Advantage Plans
  • Details of Medicare Part D Prescription Drug Coverage
  • When you are able to Enroll or Change Plans.
  • What you need to do to prepare for your transition to Medicare:
    1. Do you need to sign up for Part B? Or will it be automatic for you?
    2. Do you need a Supplemental Policy, and how do you narrow down your options?
    3. How an agent can assist you and be an invaluable asset to your Medicare experience.
    4. What you can expect in the years to come after your transition.
