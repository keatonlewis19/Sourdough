Although Sourdough techniques are most known for making Sourdough Bread, many people don't know that you can actually use your sourdough starter in a variety of baked recipes! Your starter is just a natural yeast, so realistically anything that requires yeast in it's recipe can be substituted for Sourdough Starter. You can experiment with your current recipes or search for a sourdough version of your recipe online to see how other have used it and adapt it for your own needs.

We've provided a few examples below of some different recipes that use Sourdough Starter in it to give a unique twist on some classic recipes!
